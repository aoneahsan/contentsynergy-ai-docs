# About the author

ContentSynergy AI is designed, built, and maintained by **Ahsan Mahmood** — a full-stack engineer who ships React, Capacitor, and TypeScript products with a focus on zero-cost, privacy-respecting architectures. ContentSynergy AI is one of a portfolio of free apps and open-source packages built on that principle: do the heavy work on the client, lean on free tiers, and never charge users for AI that runs on their own hardware.
