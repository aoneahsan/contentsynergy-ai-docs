# Quick Start — your first post in 5 minutes

This walkthrough takes you from a blank workspace to a scheduled post. It needs no API key and no payment — the generation runs in your browser.

## 1. Open the app and sign in

Go to [contentsynergy.aoneahsan.com](https://contentsynergy.aoneahsan.com) and sign in with Google. Signing in lets the app save your post and remember your preferences across devices. (You can explore generation without an account, but you'll want to sign in before scheduling.)

## 2. Start a text post

From the dashboard, open the content creator and choose **Text post**. The first time you do this, the browser downloads the open text model — expect a short wait once, then it's cached.

## 3. Describe what you want

Enter a short prompt (for example, "Announce our spring sale, 20% off, ends Sunday") and set the controls:

- **Tone** — professional, casual, friendly, humorous, inspirational, urgent, or informative.
- **Length** — short, medium, or long.
- **Language** — one of the supported locales.
- **Platform** — the network you're targeting, which shapes length and formatting.
- **Hashtags / emojis** — toggle on to get suggestions.

Generate. If the model is ready you'll get a model-written draft; on a constrained device you'll get a structured template draft from the same inputs. Either way you have content to work with in seconds.

## 4. Refine

Edit the draft directly, regenerate for alternatives, or apply a [fine-tuned LoRA adapter](/ai/fine-tuning) if you've trained one for your brand voice. Use the built-in readability and sentiment checks (from the [tools catalog](/tools/overview)) if you want a second opinion before publishing.

## 5. Connect a platform

Open **Publishing → Social platforms** and connect a network — Facebook, Instagram, X, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, Pinterest, or Telegram — through its OAuth 2.0 sign-in. You authorize ContentSynergy AI to post on your behalf; tokens are stored against your account. See [Social platforms](/publishing/social-platforms).

## 6. Schedule it

Open the scheduler, pick a date and time, and choose whether the post repeats (daily, weekly, monthly, or a custom cadence). Save. Your post joins the queue and the app reminds you through local notifications on mobile. See [Scheduling](/publishing/scheduling).

## Frequently asked questions

**Do I need to pay or enter a key to finish this?** No. The whole flow uses the free, browser-based AI.

**Can I post immediately instead of scheduling?** Yes — publish now or schedule for later; both use the same connected-platform tokens.

**What if generation is slow or fails?** The feature falls back to a template draft so you can keep going; the model will be faster once cached.
